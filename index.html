📋 Meet Day Checklist

What to bring to every meet

  • Swim suit, team t-shirt, team cap & goggles
  • At least 2 towels
  • Blanket or sleeping bag to sit on
  • Games, books or quiet activities
  • Snacks, water bottle & cash for concessions
  • Lawn chair for parents
  • Sunscreen & bug spray
✏️ Meet day tip: Coaches write your swimmer's Event/Heat/Lane/Stroke on their arm at practice — please rewrite before arriving at the meet!

👶 10 & under: Check in with bullpen parent on arrival. Swimmers must be in bullpen 10 events before their race.

🤒 Sick last minute? Call your team director ASAP so relay teams can be adjusted.

🎀 Ribbons handed out at Friday practice. Improvement beads awarded from meet 2 onwards!
